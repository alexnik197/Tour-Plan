What people thinks about us

Anthony Munhoz

Stayed 07 July, 2021

The charm of the hotel is mesmerizing. Every night we had a live band outside our balcony which was energetic and lively and the music was fantastic. On the boardwalk, everyone is happy. It is a joy to hear children laughing and playing, enjoying fresh air, swimming in the ocean, being kids. I am nostalgic for the Hotel Del Coronado. I would love to come back. The haunted tour in particular was the highlight of my experience. The only drawback, and the hotel has no control over this, are the two military bases sandwiching in the hotel. To the north is an obnoxious runway with bright flashing lights and noisy jets landing periodically in what can only be described as a show of force. To the south, heaven help you if you step onto the Navy's beach. (I thought beaches couldn't be private in California?) If you do, you will hear whistles, see bright lights shone on you (if it is nighttime), and you will be gestured at rudely to leave the private beach. Aside of from that buzzkill, the hotel was spectacular, the staff were friendly, warm, professional and efficient, and it was a wonderful vacation.

Emmie Fox

Stayed 18 Nov, 2019

It was very nice hotel with cleanliness. Staff behavior was good and polite. They welcome us very well. Issue was only that Lift was not in working and we were allotted to 3rd floor and amenities articles were in corner of gallery which were giving bad feeling. Breakfast was good and support of the staff was also very nice. Location is not good as per atmosphere, it is very nearby most of the popular places but self location in a narrow street is not good. Overall it was a good experience and could recommend.
